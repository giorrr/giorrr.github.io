1. Information Collection and Use

The App does not require user registration and does not directly collect any personally identifiable information. For the proper functioning and quality improvement of the App, the operating system and standard system features may automatically collect limited technical information, including:

  • Device type
  • OS version
  • App stability data (e.g., crash logs)

The App does not collect location data (including precise location). It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or photo library.

2. Data Storage

All data entered by the user (condition logs, goals, diary entries, etc.) is stored exclusively on the user's device. No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers.

Data management is delegated to the device's operating system. When the App is uninstalled, associated data is deleted according to the standard behavior of the OS.

If iCloud sync functionality is implemented in the future, data may be synchronized and stored through Apple's iCloud service, subject to the user's explicit consent. In that case, Apple's handling of your information will be governed by the Apple Privacy Policy.

3. Billing & In-App Purchases

The App offers auto-renewing subscriptions (monthly and annual plans) through Apple's App Store. All billing transactions and payment information are processed and managed by Apple. The Provider does not collect or store any payment information such as credit card numbers.

For information on how Apple handles your data, please refer to the Apple Privacy Policy.

You can manage or cancel your subscription via iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

4. Third-Party Services

The App currently does not use any third-party advertising or analytics services. If such services are introduced in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated and users will be notified in advance.

The App may use Apple's Push Notification service (APNs) to deliver local notifications. All notification content is generated and processed entirely on-device.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. The Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If it becomes apparent that such information has been collected, appropriate action will be taken promptly.

6. Security

The Provider is committed to protecting user information. The App utilizes security mechanisms provided by the OS (such as Keychain and SwiftData encryption) to safeguard information processed on-device. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage can guarantee 100% security.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of April 11, 2026.

9. Consent

By using the App, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
